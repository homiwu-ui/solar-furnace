# 003 側面結構圖 v2（修正版）

Side view (elevation) diagram of dual-axis solar tracker:

Left side: 30cm base on ground, 25cm turntable above with 5mm gap (not "25cm gap" — the gap is only 5mm, the turntable is 25cm wide)
Center: M8 threaded rod center shaft (15cm tall)
Upright columns: 20cm tall, left and right
Solar furnace: Parabolic dish shape between columns, tiltable from 0° to 80°
Horizontal motor: MG996R on base right side, 10T pinion engaging 60T turntable gear
Vertical motor: MG996R on right column, linkage to furnace rotation shaft
Counterweight: Wire + weight hanging from furnace back left side
LDR sensor positions: 4 small circles on furnace rim (left, right, top, bottom)
Key dimensions labeled: 30cm base width, 25cm turntable (not "25cm gap"), 20cm column height, 15cm shaft, 10cm focal length from dish center

Style: Clean line drawing with light color fill, dimension lines with arrows, 1:10 scale indication, white background, suitable for children's instruction manual. Labels in Traditional Chinese.

IMPORTANT: The gap between base and turntable is 5mm, not 25cm. 25cm is the turntable width.
